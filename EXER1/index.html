ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Allyssa Albores. I am a Computer Science student at the University of the Philippines Cebu. I am an aspiring web developer and currently taking a theoretical computer science specialization. It is my personal goal in the next years to become a professional in my field. Now, I am still gathering work experiences and have something great I can put on my resume.

I have worked very hard to get the knowledge I currently have, but I'm just as passionate about where this is leading me. The challenges that I deal with on a day-to-day basis I find meaningful and rewarding. Bottom line: I honestly love what I do and I will do whatever it takes to further my career.

Besides being a programmer, I love travelling and taking pictures. One reason I want to be good in my field is to earn enough money for travelling. It has always been my dream to travel the world using my hard-earned money. I believe that the gladdest moment in human life is a departure into unknown lands.
